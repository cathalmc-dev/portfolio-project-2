Welcome to the Countdown Numbers Game!

Instructions

  • Choose any 6 cards from the below. The top 4 are large numbers and the bottom 20 are small numbers.
  • Stop the spinner to generate a random 3 digit target number.
  • Add, subtract, multiply or divide your 6 numbers to get as close to the target number as you can. Each number may only be used once.
  • 10 points for getting the number exactly, 7 for being within 5 of it and 5 for being within 10. Any further away is 0 points.
